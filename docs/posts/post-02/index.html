  • Maturity: Importance for yield and volatility.

  • Liquidity and Inflation Risks: Challenges of selling and erosion of value.

  • Risks and Rewards: Higher yields, complex structures.

HFIS-60: Financing Positions in the Bond Market

  • Instruments: Repos, dollar rolls, securities lending.
